Frequently Asked Questions

Is SmartTripPlanner AI free to use?

Yes, completely free. You can generate up to 10 itineraries per day without signing in. Sign in with your Google account to save your trips and access your dashboard.

How accurate are the AI-generated itineraries?

Our itineraries are an excellent starting point for trip planning, but AI-generated content should always be verified. Always confirm current flight prices, hotel availability, visa requirements, and local conditions from official sources before booking.

Can I suggest a new feature or destination?

Absolutely! We love hearing from our users. Send us a message via the form above and select “General Enquiry” or “Other” as the subject. We read every message.

How can I partner or collaborate with SmartTripPlanner AI?

For affiliate partnerships, brand collaborations, sponsored content, or media enquiries, please select “Partnership / Collaboration” in the form subject dropdown and describe your proposal. We’ll get back to you within 48 hours.
