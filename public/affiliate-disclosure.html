Our commitment to transparency — Last updated: April 4, 2026

1. Our Commitment to Transparency

📢 Important Notice: SmartTripPlanner AI participates in affiliate marketing programs. When you click certain links on our site and make a purchase or booking, we may earn a commission — at no additional cost to you. Your support through these links helps keep our platform free for everyone.

We believe in full transparency with our users. This page explains exactly how our affiliate relationships work, which partners we work with, and how it may (or may not) affect the content we produce.

2. What Is Affiliate Marketing?

Affiliate marketing is a revenue model where a website earns a commission by recommending products or services. When you click an affiliate link on our site and complete a booking or purchase, the retailer or service provider pays us a small referral fee. This commission comes from the retailer’s own marketing budget — it does not add any cost to your transaction.

This model allows us to offer our AI travel planning service completely free of charge to all users.

3. Our Affiliate Partners

Hotel Bookings

We include links to hotels on platforms such as Booking.com. When you click “View on Booking.com” links within your AI-generated itinerary and complete a hotel booking, we may receive an affiliate commission from Booking.com. The price you pay is the same as booking directly through Booking.com.

Flight Bookings

Flight estimate links and flight search tools featured on our itinerary and result pages may be affiliate-powered. When you search for or book flights through these links on partner platforms, we may earn a referral fee. All flight prices shown in itineraries are AI-estimated approximations; actual prices depend on availability and market rates.

Activities & Experiences

Links to tours, activities, day trips, and local experiences recommended in your itinerary may be affiliate links via platforms such as GetYourGuide, Viator, and Klook. When you book an activity through these links, we may earn a small commission.

Travel Insurance & Other Services

We may from time to time recommend travel insurance providers, international SIM card services, or travel accessories via affiliate partnerships. These recommendations will be clearly presented and any affiliate relationship will be evident from context.

4. Does This Affect Our Recommendations?

We take editorial independence very seriously. Here’s our commitment to you:

  • Our AI generates itineraries and hotel recommendations based solely on relevance to your destination and travel preferences — not on affiliate commission rates.
  • We do not inflate prices, manipulate search rankings, or recommend inferior products simply because they offer higher commissions.
  • Our AI travel content is generated by Google Gemini and is not influenced by commercial relationships.
  • Affiliate income helps keep SmartTripPlanner AI free. Our goal is always to give you the best possible travel plan.

5. No Additional Cost to You

✅ Affiliate commissions come from the retailer’s marketing budget — not from you. You never pay more because you used our links. In fact, our affiliate partnerships sometimes give us access to exclusive rates or deals that we can pass on to you.

6. How to Identify Affiliate Links

Affiliate links on our site typically direct you to third-party booking platforms such as Booking.com, GetYourGuide, Viator, or Klook. These links function as normal hyperlinks and take you directly to the product or service on the partner platform. We do not use deceptive link masking, cloaking, or misleading anchor text.

In blog posts and content pages, affiliate links may be disclosed with a note such as “(affiliate link)” or “(we may earn a commission)” where applicable.
