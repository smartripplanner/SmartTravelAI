2. What Is Affiliate Marketing?

Affiliate marketing is a revenue model where a website earns a commission by recommending products or services. When you click an affiliate link and complete a booking or purchase, the retailer or service provider pays a small referral fee. This commission comes from the retailer’s own marketing budget — it does not add any cost to your transaction.

This model allows us to offer our AI travel planning service completely free of charge to all users.
