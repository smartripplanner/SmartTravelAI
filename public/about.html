Our Story

SmartTripPlanner AI was born from a simple frustration: planning a trip takes hours of research, endless browser tabs, and still leaves you unsure if you’ve made the best choices. Piecing together an itinerary, comparing hotels, estimating budgets, checking visa requirements, and figuring out transport — it’s overwhelming.

We built SmartTripPlanner AI to change that. By harnessing the power of Google Gemini AI, we generate complete, personalized travel itineraries in seconds — with day-by-day plans, hotel picks, interactive maps, budget breakdowns, visa guidance, and packing lists all included.

No more tab overload. No more guesswork. Just your perfect trip, planned in under 30 seconds.

How It Works

We use Google Gemini, one of the world’s most capable AI language models, to understand your travel preferences and generate contextually rich, personalized itineraries. Behind the scenes, our platform integrates:

  • Google Places API — for real hotel images, location data, and place details
  • Google Maps — for interactive, day-by-day route mapping with directions
  • Open-Meteo & weather APIs — for destination weather context
  • Live currency conversion — to display budgets in your preferred currency
  • AI Chatbot — to answer follow-up questions about your specific itinerary
  • Firebase — for secure account management and trip saving
