Your Privacy Matters to Us

Last updated: April 4, 2026  ·  Effective: April 4, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to SmartTripPlanner AI (“we”, “our”, or “us”). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, when we share it, and what rights you have in relation to it.

By using SmartTripPlanner AI at smartripplannerai.netlify.app, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you have concerns, please contact us before using our service.

2. Information We Collect

Information You Provide Directly

  • Account information — your name and email address when you sign in via Google OAuth. We do not store or have access to your Google password.
  • Travel preferences — destination, travel dates, budget, group size, and trip style entered when generating an itinerary.
  • Newsletter subscription — your email address if you choose to subscribe to our newsletter.
  • Contact form submissions — your name, email, and message when you contact us.

Automatically Collected Information

  • IP address and approximate geographic location
  • Browser type, version, and language settings
  • Operating system and device type
  • Pages visited, session duration, and navigation paths
  • Referring URLs and exit pages
  • Date and time of your visit

Travel Planning Data

Destination, travel dates, budget, and group size you provide when generating an itinerary are processed to create your trip plan. This data may be retained to allow you to access saved trips via your dashboard. We do not sell this data to third parties.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To generate personalized AI-powered travel itineraries based on your inputs
  • To send your itinerary to your email when you use the “Email Itinerary” feature
  • To save and display your trips in your personal dashboard (when signed in)
  • To improve our AI models, features, and overall website experience
  • To send occasional travel tips and updates (only if you subscribed to our newsletter)
  • To respond to your support requests and enquiries
  • To monitor usage patterns and prevent abuse of our service
  • To comply with applicable legal obligations

4. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function correctly. They include session cookies that maintain your login state and theme preferences (dark/light mode). You cannot opt out of essential cookies without affecting site functionality.

Analytics — Google Analytics 4

We use Google Analytics 4 (Property ID: G-8T2Y0ZGS30) to understand how visitors interact with our website. Data collected is aggregated and anonymized. You can opt out via the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on. See Google’s Privacy Policy for more details.

Behaviour Analytics — Microsoft Clarity

We use Microsoft Clarity (Project ID: w612r8bafr) to record anonymized heatmaps and session replays. Clarity may record mouse movements, clicks, and scrolls to help us improve user experience. No personally identifiable information is collected without consent. See Microsoft’s Privacy Statement for details.

Third-Party Cookies

Affiliate partners (e.g., Booking.com) may set their own cookies when you click booking links. These are governed by the respective platform’s privacy policies, which we encourage you to review.

Managing Cookies

You can control and delete cookies via your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect some website functionality. Visit allaboutcookies.org for guidance on managing cookies across different browsers.

5. Email Communications

  • We may send your generated itinerary to your email when you use the “Email Itinerary” feature on the results page.
  • If you subscribed to our newsletter, you can unsubscribe at any time using the link in any email we send.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your email address with third parties for their marketing purposes.
  • Transactional emails related to your account are managed through Firebase/Google.

6. Affiliate Links & Third Parties

Our website contains affiliate links to hotel booking platforms (e.g., Booking.com), flight search tools, and activity providers. When you click these links, you are directed to third-party websites that operate under their own privacy policies. We are not responsible for the data practices of these third-party sites. See our Affiliate Disclosure for how these relationships work.

7. Data Storage & Retention

  • User account data and saved trips are stored securely using Firebase (Google Cloud) with industry-standard encryption and security controls.
  • Trip data is retained until you delete your account or request its deletion.
  • Analytics data is retained per the default retention periods of Google Analytics and Microsoft Clarity.
  • Contact form submissions may be retained for up to 12 months for support purposes.

8. Data Security

We implement security measures including HTTPS encryption for all data in transit, Firebase security rules governing access to your trip data, and Google OAuth for secure authentication. We do not store payment information. While we take every reasonable precaution, no method of internet transmission is 100% secure.

9. Your Rights

  • Access — Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction — Request correction of inaccurate personal data.
  • Deletion — Request deletion of your account and associated data via your Profile page or by contacting us.
  • Opt-out — Unsubscribe from newsletter emails at any time or opt out of analytics tracking.
  • Data portability — Request your data in a portable format where applicable.

EU/EEA Users: You have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), including the right to lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority.

Indian Users: You have rights under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDPA), including the right to access, correct, and erase your personal data.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us.

10. Children’s Privacy

SmartTripPlanner AI is not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately and we will delete it promptly.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the “Last updated” date at the top of this page. Your continued use of our service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
